About us

Our purpose is to inform you about the situation of Sitio Bebe. Sitio Bebe
faces a problem where poor water supply due to not being near to a
natural water source, diseases that are the source of their jobs, which is
mostly mining, no colleges that have a health expert course which leads
to short staff in clinics, and with Covid-19 where the local government
focuses on the pandemic which leaves the prior problems at hold for
solutions. You, a youth, a school head, and a donator, can help the
residents of Sitio Bebe, by joining our concert which will help donate funds
for the prior concerns of Sitio Bebe.
